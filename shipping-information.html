Shipping Information

We ship all throughout Indonesia!

Packages are guaranteed to be dispatched to our shipping carriers within 2 working days after payment confirmation.

Shipping fees include handling and transport fees. Handling fees are fixed, while transport fees vary depending on the total weight of the shipment, as well as total distance to the destination.

We will provide protective cases for your products to ensure that your items are safe and well-protected.

Delivery time depends on the distance to the destination. For estimated time of arrival, please do check on our shipping carrier's website using the shipment tracking number. Shipment tracking numbers will be sent to your email within 1 day of dispatch.

We ship our products from South Tangerang.
