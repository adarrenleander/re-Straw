About Our Company

"Be a part of the solution, not part of the pollution."

re:Straw is a result of collective ideas from three college students, which focuses on the negative effects of using primitive plastic straws. Regarding the ideas, we talked about how plastic straws are used anually all over the globe and wondered if straws are actually needed. Surprisingly, the time that a straw is actually used is only about fifteen minutes. A short amount of time, compared to the time that it is actually degraded.

Plastic straws made up almost 5 percent of total plastic waste weighing around two thousand tons of the nearly nine million tons of plastic waste anually. Although straws don't add significant amount of plastic waste, they greatly endangers many species of animals. The small shape of straw and its little weight can deceive animals to consume the plastic. Because of these matters, we decided to create eco-friendly straws that can be used repeatedly. We hope that by selling these straws, all of us can be a part of contributon to reduce plastic waste.
