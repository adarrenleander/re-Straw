ABOUT OUR PRODUCTS

Our main products in re:Straw are reusable straws which have the durability and simple to use. The focus of our product is to reduce the usage of plastic, hence we use different materials for our straws. Four main materials are used to make the straws, with variation of colors and height. We used glass, metal, bamboo and even durable paper as substitute for plastic. Our materials are guaranteed to be durable and lightweight so that our customers can feel comfortable when bringing around the straw.

Will we expand our products? In the near future, we will develop more and more products which are made from eco-friendly materials. So, don't forget to sign up and get notified about our new releases!
